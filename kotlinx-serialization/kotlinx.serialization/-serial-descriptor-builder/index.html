SerialDescriptorBuilder

(common, js, jvm, native) class SerialDescriptorBuilder

Builder for SerialDescriptor. Both explicit builder functions and implicit (using typeOf) are present and are equivalent. For example, element<Int?>("nullableIntField") is indistinguishable from element("nullableIntField", IntSerializer.descriptor.nullable) and from element("nullableIntField", descriptor<Int?>).

Please refer to SerialDescriptor builder function for a complete example.
